CHRIST ( DEEMED TO BE UNIVERSITY )

The IEEE STUDENT BODY, Student Branch Chapter of CHRIST(Deemed to be University) is an initiative by the students from Christ (Deemed to be University). It strives to provide a learning environment for our students to enhance and uplift their knowledge and improve their networking and to create individuals suited for the industry. The student branch focuses on achieving its objectives by conducting social and technical events like hands-on workshops on various emerging technologies, seminars, lectures, guest talks and many other programs.

It also aspires to provide various opportunities for students to form a strong network with peers, academicians, scientists, professionals; thereby motivating students to be a part of the global IEEE.The student branch encourages the students to take full advantage of the benefits of IEEE membership.We welcome our students and all interested members to come to be a part of our community and benefit from our endeavours!
